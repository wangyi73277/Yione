Odyssey to Peter Cook’s Fruity City is inspired by a series of visionary drawings by British architect Peter Cook depicting an imaginary future city known as Fruity City.

A young traveler discovers a hidden, idyllic utopia. Through the traveler’s journey, Fruity City unfolds as a vibrant landscape where architecture, nature, and light merge into a surreal urban organism. Elevated walkways, luminous structures, and playful spatial compositions reveal a city imagined with the boundless optimism of the late twentieth century.

As a vision of a future once imagined in the past, Fruity City invites us to reflect on how earlier generations envisioned the world we inhabit today.
