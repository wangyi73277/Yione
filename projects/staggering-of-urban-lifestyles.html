Staggering of Urban Lifestyles

Year: 2025
Type: High-rise Affordable Housing
Design Team: Yi Wang, Wentao Guo

story

×

Staggering of Urban Lifestyles is an innovative prefab residential high-rise that employs staggered steel trusses in askew configurations and varying spans to create a lightweight, cost-effective, and rapidly erectable structure. This system enables open, flexible living space suited for diverse lifestyles. Strategically floating above an extention of the Williamsburg Waterfront park, the massing responds to zoning, wind, and floodplain conditions. Its modular construction, varied unit types, and inserted public amenities foster contextual, community-centered urban living.
