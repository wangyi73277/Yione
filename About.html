Yione Studio is a spatial design practice based in New York, USA. The studio operates at the intersection of architecture, landscape, and emerging technologies, with a mission to craft meaningful, transformative environments across scales.

Team
Yi Wang AIA, RIBA, NOMA, LEED Green Associate
Yi Wang is a registered architect and interdisciplinary designer based in New York. She holds a Master of Architecture degree from Washington University in St. Louis and has previously worked at internationally renowned architectural offices, including Christian Kerez GmbH, SO–IL, Dorte Mandrup Arkitekter A/S, and Kengo Kuma & Associates.
Her work explores the intersections of architecture, art, and technology, emphasizing cultural memory, urban narratives, and sustainable design strategies. Currently, she continues to investigate how architecture can engage and transform public perception and urban experiences.

Xiaochang Qiu
Xiaochang Qiu is a dedicated landscape architect who combines her lifelong passion for nature with a focus on multi-sensorial design. She earned her Master of Landscape Architecture degree from Cornell University and has practiced at renowned firms such as AECOM and BIG.
With a strong belief in the intersectionality of landscapes, Xiaochang aims to create spaces that promote biodiversity, mitigate climate change, and enhance overall well-being. She also recognizes the vital role of landscape architecture in shaping a more equitable and just world. Through her designs, she strives to contribute to a more sustainable and inclusive future.
